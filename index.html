Tom And Jerry

Tom and Jerry is a classic animated series created by Hanna and Barbera in 1940. It follows the comedic rivalry between Tom, a cat, and Jerry, a mouse, using slapstick humor and physical comedy. The series is wordless, beloved worldwide, and has won several Academy Awards.
